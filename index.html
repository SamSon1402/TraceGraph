Three schemas in. One trajectory out.

Agent training data isn't documents — it's trajectories: the ordered chain of who did what, across which tools, referencing what. TraceGraph ingests raw exports from Slack, Jira and Notion (each with its own schema), normalizes them into one event model, links the cross-references, and reconstructs the multi-step workflow an agent would actually learn from.
